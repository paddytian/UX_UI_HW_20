Overview

Description

Simcoe & District Humane Society is a non-profit organization that receives no government funding or assistance and relies solely on the donations and fundraisers provided by volunteers. Their mission statement is to help animals & people in Norfolk County and treat animals that are unwanted, abandoned or otherwise cannot be cared for due to overpopulation.

Pain Points

User Persona

Simmy Rodriguez, a shy and reserved woman, wants to adopt a pet to suit her more indoorsy lifestyle, because she wishes for a form of companionship that leaves her less anxious and lonely.

Problem Statement

Simcoe And District Humane Society was designed to achieve the goal of pet adoption and volunteer services. We have observed that the website isn’t meeting the goal of pet adoption and volunteer services due to an inconsistent navigation, which is discouraging users from adopting pets from the Simcoe and District Humane Society, or even volunteering and donating.

Design Solution
01
Organized Navigation Bar

We reorganized the navigation bar. The main features for the website are easy to find now. And we also create a search bar, the users could transfer to the website they want easily.

02
Online fill form

The original forms are pdf fill, and the user should download and email to them. We create an online fill form and they can also book for visit after they fill out the application form.

03
Donation page

We create a new donation page. We delete the email transfer and phone call payment method, and add credit card payment. Users could choose the amount and also choose the different payment from credit card or PayPal.
